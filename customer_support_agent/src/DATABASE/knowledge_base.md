# Glowmart Knowledge Base
**Brand:** Glowmart | Beauty & Home Decor E-Commerce (India)

---

## 1. About Glowmart
Glowmart is a premium online store selling beauty products (skincare, makeup,
haircare) and home decor (scented candles, diffusers, wall art, cushion covers).
We ship across India. All products are cruelty-free and dermatologist-tested
where applicable. Founded in 2022, Glowmart serves over 50,000 customers.

---

## 2. Shipping Policy
- Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days. Free above ₹999.
- Express Delivery: 2-3 business days. ₹99 flat fee.
- Same Day Delivery: Available in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore only. ₹149 fee.
- Orders placed before 2 PM IST are dispatched the same day.
- A tracking link is sent via SMS and email once dispatched.
- We do not currently ship internationally.

---

## 3. Return and Refund Policy
- Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery.
- Products must be unused and in original packaging.
- Opened beauty products are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
- To initiate a return: email support@glowmart.in with your order ID.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after return pickup.
- Refunds are credited to the original payment method only.
- Exchanges are available for size or color issues on home decor items.

---

## 4. Payment Methods
- UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
- Credit and Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, RuPay
- Net Banking
- EMI available on orders above ₹2,000 (select banks)
- Cash on Delivery available on orders below ₹3,000

---

## 5. Order Cancellation
- Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement.
- After dispatch, cancellation is not possible. Raise a return request instead.
- To cancel: go to My Orders → Select Order → Cancel.

---

## 6. Beauty Products
- Skincare: Moisturizers, serums, sunscreens, face wash, eye cream
- Makeup: Lipsticks, foundations, kajal, blush, highlighter
- Haircare: Oils, shampoos, conditioners, hair masks
- All ingredient lists are available on each product page.
- Patch test is recommended before using new skincare products.

## 7. Home Decor Products
- Scented candles: Soy wax, 40-hour average burn time
- Reed diffusers: 30-day fragrance life, available in 6 scents
- Wall art: Available in A4, A3, A2 sizes
- Cushion covers: Machine washable, 40x40 cm standard size

## 8. GlowPoints Loyalty Program
- Earn 1 GlowPoint per ₹10 spent.
- 100 GlowPoints = ₹10 discount on your next order.
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.
- Double points during your birthday month.
- Check your balance: My Account → GlowPoints section.
